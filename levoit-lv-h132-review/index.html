3-step filtration

The Levoit LV-H132 air purifier contains a three-stage filtration system, which will help remove a wide range of particles and cure various problems such as unpleasant odours, smoke or pet odours.

FILTRATION SYSTEM

Given the dimensions of the air purifier, the amount of filtration phases is striking.

  1. Fine Pre-Filter — This filter covers all large particles flying in the air. Although the TrueHEPA filter can also treat them, large contaminants shorten the life of the filter. That’s why a pre-filter saves money. It protects airborne pathogens, large fungal spores, animal hair, etc. The pre-filter is not washable and therefore does not try to do this. You may damage the other filters. Change the pre-filter and the different filters every 6 to 9 weeks.

 

  1. TrueHEPA Filter – This filter treats 99.97% of particles with a diameter of 0.3 microns. This means that it protects the allergens, pollen and animal skin scales that make your life miserable. Air purifiers equipped with trueHEPA filters are an excellent choice for all environments. This filter should be replaced approximately every 6 to 9 weeks.

 

  1. Active carbon filter — This filter creates odours. Therefore, if you have a smoker in your home, prefer to cook dishes with exotic smells, such as large pets or other fragrances to take care of, this charcoal filter can treat them quickly. The filter should be changed every 6 to 9 weeks, depending on how often the device is used.
