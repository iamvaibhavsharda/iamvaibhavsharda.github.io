What is the difference between a mold air purifier and a dehumidifier?

A dehumidifier removes moisture from the air. An air purifier does not treat humidity. On the contrary, it brings air to the filter, eliminates airborne spores and releases clean, clean air. A dehumidifier has a bucket that collects moisture (it can be alarming to see exactly how far away).
