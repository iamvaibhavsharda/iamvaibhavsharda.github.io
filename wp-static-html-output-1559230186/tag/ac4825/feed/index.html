How difficult can it be to change the filter?

Filter replacement is easy and only takes a few seconds. I was pleasantly surprised with how easy it is.

Basically, it’s just a few simple steps:

  1. Press the release latch on the back of the cover.
  2. Pull the cover down and remove it (there are 2 tabs on the bottom of the slots).
  3. Pull out the filter with your fingers and remove it by pulling it down.
  4. Replace the filter with the opposite movement.
  5. Place the cover tabs on the bottom.
  6. Turn the cover upwards and replace the release latch.

Why is the blue or red light flashing when I turn on the air purifier and how can I turn it off?

The air purifier comes with indicator lights that blink automatically when it is time to change the UV-C filter or lamp.
